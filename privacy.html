1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Learny ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal data when you use our AI Czech language tutoring service ("Service").

We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in accordance with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Data Controller

The data controller responsible for your personal data is:

  • Name: Darya Tulegaliyeva
  • Email: support@learny.cz
  • Website: learny.cz

3. Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of data when you use the Service:

3.1 Telegram Account Data

  • Telegram user ID (numeric identifier)
  • Username (if set)
  • Display name (first name and last name, if provided)
  • Language preference settings

3.2 Conversation Data

  • Text messages you send to the bot
  • Voice messages and their transcriptions
  • Bot responses and conversation history

3.3 Learning Data

  • Language level assessment results
  • Error correction history
  • Learning progress and topic preferences

3.4 Usage Data

  • Message counts and frequency
  • Daily streak information
  • Feature usage statistics
  • Dates and times of interactions

3.5 Payment Data

Payment processing is handled by Tribute.tg. We do not collect, store, or have access to your credit card numbers, bank account details, or other financial information. Tribute.tg may share with us:

  • Subscription status (active, cancelled, expired)
  • Payment confirmation and transaction IDs
  • Email address (if provided during checkout)

4. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: To provide AI-powered Czech language tutoring, process your messages, and generate personalized responses
  • Personalization: To assess your language level, adapt content difficulty, and track your learning progress
  • Error correction: To analyze your messages for language errors and provide helpful corrections
  • Service improvement: To understand usage patterns and improve the quality of our AI tutoring
  • Communication: To send you important service updates, billing notifications, and respond to support requests
  • Abuse prevention: To detect and prevent misuse of the Service

5. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data based on the following legal grounds under the GDPR:

  • Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b)): Processing necessary to deliver the Service you have subscribed to
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): Where you have given explicit consent, such as starting the bot and sending messages
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)): For service improvement, analytics, and abuse prevention, balanced against your rights and freedoms
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)): Where we are required to retain data by law

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for the following periods:

  • Conversation history: Retained while your account is active, deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
  • Learning progress data: Retained while your account is active
  • Voice messages: Processed in real-time for transcription and not stored after processing
  • Usage statistics: Retained in aggregated, anonymized form indefinitely for service improvement
  • Billing records: Retained as required by applicable tax and accounting laws (typically up to 10 years)

When you request account deletion, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

8. Your Rights

Under the GDPR and applicable data protection laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to data portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to restrict processing: Request that we limit how we use your data
  • Right to object: Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@learny.cz. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority, such as the Czech Office for Personal Data Protection (UOOU).

9. International Data Transfers

Some of our third-party processors (OpenAI, Tribute.tg, Railway) are located in the United States. When your data is transferred outside the European Economic Area (EEA), we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

  • EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework certification
  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission

10. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 16. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 16, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at support@learny.cz.
