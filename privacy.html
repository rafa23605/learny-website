1. Who We Are

Learny is an AI-powered Czech language tutor that runs inside Telegram. It's operated by Learny ("we," "us," "our").

We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains exactly what data we collect when you use Learny, why we collect it, and what your rights are. If anything here is unclear, email us at support@learny.cz â€” we're happy to explain.

  • Email: support@learny.cz
  • Website: language-teach.com

2. What Data We Collect

Telegram account data

When you start the bot, Telegram shares your basic profile with us:

  • Telegram user ID (a numeric identifier)
  • Username, if you have one set
  • First name and last name, if provided in your Telegram profile

Conversation data

  • Text messages you send to the bot
  • Voice messages and their transcriptions
  • The bot's responses

Learning data

  • Your language level (from the placement test)
  • Correction history and progress
  • Topic preferences and daily streak data

Subscription & payment data

We use Lemon Squeezy as our Merchant of Record (MoR) to handle all payments. We never see your full card number or bank credentials. Lemon Squeezy may share with us:

  • Whether your subscription is active, cancelled, or expired
  • Transaction confirmation and order IDs
  • Email address used at checkout, if provided
  • Country and tax metadata required for invoicing and compliance

3. How We Use It

  • To run the service: Process your messages, generate responses, and deliver your Czech lessons
  • To personalise your experience: Adapt lesson difficulty to your level and track your progress
  • To correct your Czech: Analyse your messages and provide accurate corrections with explanations in Ukrainian
  • To manage your subscription: Know whether your account is in trial, active, or expired
  • To improve Learny: Understand how the service is used so we can make it better
  • To keep the service safe: Detect and prevent abuse or misuse

We do not sell your data. We do not use it for advertising. We do not share it with anyone except the processors listed below.

4. Legal Basis

We process your data under the following GDPR grounds:

  • Performance of a contract (Art. 6(1)(b)): To deliver the service you subscribed to
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)): When you start the bot and send messages
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)): For service improvement and fraud prevention
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c)): Where required by law (e.g. tax records)

6. How Long We Keep It

  • Conversation and learning data: Kept while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of an account deletion request.
  • Voice messages: Transcribed in real time and not stored afterwards.
  • Usage statistics: Kept in anonymised, aggregated form indefinitely to improve the service.
  • Billing records: Kept as long as required by Czech and EU tax law (typically 10 years).
